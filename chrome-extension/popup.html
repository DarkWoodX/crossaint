Talk-To-ChatGPT

To start using this extension, you need to open the ChatGPT home page. Once you are logged in, a box will display at the top right corner of your screen with a 'START' button. Click START to start talking.

This extension allows you to talk to ChatGPT with your microphone, using Google Chrome's voice recognition API. Then, when your browser receives the reply, it is read out loud with a voice of your choosing.
